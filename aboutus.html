PEACE-The College Of Science & Management

Genesis-Trinity is one of the India's elite business schools. Genesis-Trinity academic strength drives original and provocative business thinking, empowering our students to challenge conventional wisdom in a truly unique academic environment. Genesis-Trinity believes in building a Designer Persona in each student which is customized as per their capacity, dreams and aspirations. With this motive, Genesis-Trinity was established in the year 2008. It is one of the fastest growing colleges of India with a commitment to have state-of-the-art infrastructure, world class teaching faculty and enriched library. The facilities include Cafeteria, Cricket Stadium, Health club, Wifi enabled computer lab and campus.
